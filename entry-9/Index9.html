9.Chickens

I just...it's what I'm getting ok? Bird Brain! It's an actual thing

Interesting Facts→

  1. Chickens are living descendants of dinosaurs.
  2. Chickens can dream.
  3. Chickens have better color vision than humans.
  4. Chickens have great memories.
  5. Chickens bathe by covering themselves in dirt.
  6. Chickens have complex communication.

Chickens are notorious­ly un­intelligent; they're best known for having bodies that can function without even having heads attached to them.

Even while some chicken owners would try to claim that chickens have a lot of emotional and innate intel­ligence, that is almost rarely the case. In the Northwest, a chicken owner confessed, "I don't believe I had any un­reasonable expect­ations here, but I've been taken away by exactly how not-smart chickens are. I've decided your average chicken is packing about as much processing power as a VIC-20. You know, 5 kilobytes of RAM and a BASIC interpreter.”

They reiterate how much they love the animal... and so essential­ly, this is basically equivalent to a parent calling their own child stupid.

Chickens exhibit a wide range of idiocy, much like people. In their own gated coops, some hens can go lost. Some of the more stupid ones have a tendency to be highly co­-dependent and become agitated when sepa­rated from the others. The thing that makes them particular­ly stupid, though, is that they manage to lose the rest of the flock in a coop that is a healthy size (8"x12").

The poultry farmer is quoted as saying, "I see her, stumbling around and ba-gawking for her friends, who are about 5 feet from her. It's astounding.

When one of its flock is attacked, a chicken may know to flee and hide, but it kinda rarely recognizes that it is being chased until it is too late.

Although they are great instances of herding behavior in animals, chickens are not always the intelligent, independent thinkers that other animals may be.

Conclusion

I can say with certainty that the Chicken Run movies are not documentaries. They are never going to be able to execute a planned escape attempt, they could not possibly construct a nearly finished mechanical flying chicken and hide it within the coop, and they most likely do not have a Scottish accent.
